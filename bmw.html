Project Overview

Business Management World is a digital platform designed to provide business leaders, managers, and entrepreneurs with actionable insights, strategic frameworks, and management tools. The platform aims to bridge the gap between academic business theories and practical implementation in real-world scenarios.

My Role

Lead UI/UX Designer, Information Architect, Content Strategist

Timeline

8 weeks (Discovery to High-Fidelity Prototype)

Tools Used

Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, Miro, Google Analytics

The Challenge

Business professionals struggle to find reliable, practical business management resources that are both comprehensive and easily digestible. Existing platforms either offer overly academic content or superficial advice without depth.

Key Problems

  • Fragmented information across multiple sources
  • Lack of practical implementation guidance
  • Poor user experience in existing platforms
  • No personalized content recommendations
  • Inadequate mobile experience

Business Goals

  • Create a trusted resource for business professionals
  • Establish subscription-based revenue model
  • Build community engagement
  • Position as thought leadership platform

Key Features & Solutions

Personalized Dashboard

Customizable dashboard showing relevant content based on user role, interests, and reading history with progress tracking for learning paths.

Interactive Toolkits

Downloadable templates, calculators, and frameworks that users can immediately apply to their business challenges.

Expert Insights

Curated content from industry leaders and subject matter experts with practical implementation guidance.

Case Study Library

Searchable database of real-world business case studies filterable by industry, company size, and challenge type.

Learning Paths

Structured learning journeys for specific business skills like leadership, strategy, and financial management.

Community Forum

Professional networking and knowledge sharing space for business leaders to connect and collaborate.

Key Learnings

Content Strategy is Crucial

The success of the platform heavily depended on having the right mix of content types and ensuring practical applicability.

Mobile-First Essential

Business professionals consume content on-the-go, making mobile experience non-negotiable for engagement.

Community Drives Retention

The networking and community features significantly increased user retention and platform stickiness.
