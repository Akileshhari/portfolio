SouthCraft Handcrafting Website

Creating an authentic e-commerce platform for traditional South Indian handicrafts with modern user experience

View Prototype Back to Portfolio

Project Overview

SouthCraft is an e-commerce platform dedicated to preserving and promoting authentic South Indian handicrafts. The project aimed to create a digital marketplace that showcases the rich cultural heritage while providing a seamless shopping experience for customers worldwide.

My Role

UI/UX Designer, Product Designer, User Researcher

Timeline

8 weeks (Discovery to High-Fidelity Prototype)

Tools Used

Figma, Adobe XD, Photoshop, Illustrator

The Challenge

Traditional handicraft businesses face significant challenges in the digital space:

  • Limited online presence and digital marketing
  • Difficulty showcasing product authenticity and craftsmanship
  • Complex navigation and poor product discovery
  • Lack of trust in online transactions
  • Inadequate mobile shopping experience

"We have amazing products, but we struggle to reach customers who appreciate authentic handicrafts. Most e-commerce platforms don't showcase the story behind our crafts."

-Rajesh Kumar Traditional Wood Carver from Tamil Nadu

User Research & Discovery

I conducted comprehensive research to understand both artisans and customers:

Target Audience

  • Artisans & Craft Communities (Ages 25-65)
  • Cultural Enthusiasts (Ages 30-55)
  • Interior Designers & Collectors
  • Tourists & Cultural Explorers

Key Insights

  • 92% want to know the artisan's story
  • 78% prefer high-quality product images
  • 85% need detailed product descriptions
  • 67% want secure payment options
  • 73% shop on mobile devices

User Personas

Priya, 35 - Interior Designer

Goals: Find unique, authentic pieces for clients. Understand craftsmanship and materials.

Frustrations: Poor product images, lack of artisan information, difficult navigation.

Arun, 42 - Cultural Collector

Goals: Build collection of traditional crafts. Learn about cultural significance.

Frustrations: Inauthentic products, limited selection, poor customer service.

Key Features

Artisan Stories

Dedicated sections showcasing artisan backgrounds, techniques, and cultural significance of each craft.

Advanced Filtering

Filter by region, craft type, materials, price range, and artisan to find perfect products.

High-Quality Visuals

360° product views, detailed close-ups, and lifestyle images to showcase craftsmanship.

Secure Checkout

Multiple payment options, SSL encryption, and transparent return policies.

Mobile-First Design

Responsive design optimized for mobile shopping with intuitive touch interactions.

Cultural Blog

Educational content about South Indian crafts, traditions, and cultural significance.

User Flow & Information Architecture

Primary User Journey

Homepage → Product Discovery → Product Details → Artisan Story → Add to Cart → Secure Checkout → Order Confirmation

Key User Flows

  • Product Discovery: Browse by category → Filter results → View product details
  • Artisan Connection: Select product → Read artisan story → Learn about craft → Purchase
  • Mobile Shopping: Responsive browsing → Easy navigation → Quick checkout
