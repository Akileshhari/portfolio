Chennai Metro Application

Redesigning the Chennai Metro app for seamless travel planning, easy booking, and real-time train tracking

View Prototype Back to Portfolio

Project Overview

The Chennai Metro application needed a complete redesign to improve user experience, simplify the booking process, and provide real-time information about train schedules and locations. The goal was to create an intuitive interface that would make metro travel more accessible and convenient for both regular commuters and occasional travelers.

My Role

UI/UX Designer, User Researcher, Prototyper

Timeline

6 weeks (Research to High-Fidelity Prototype)

Tools Used

Figma, Adobe XD, Photoshop, Illustrator

The Challenge

The existing Chennai Metro app had several usability issues:

  • Complex navigation and booking process
  • Lack of real-time train information
  • No GPS tracking for train locations
  • Poor visual design and inconsistent UI elements
  • Limited payment options

User Research

I conducted interviews with 15 regular metro users and surveyed 50 occasional travelers to understand their pain points and needs:

Key Findings

  • 85% wanted real-time train tracking
  • 72% found booking tickets confusing
  • 68% wanted fare information before booking
  • 63% preferred digital tickets over physical ones
  • 58% wanted notifications for delays

User Personas

Based on research, I created two primary user personas:

  • Rahul (28, IT Professional): Daily commuter who values efficiency and real-time updates
  • Priya (45, Teacher): Occasional traveler who needs clear guidance and simple booking

Design Process

1. Research

User interviews, surveys, competitive analysis

2. Ideation

Brainstorming, user flows, information architecture

3. Wireframing

Low-fidelity sketches and digital wireframes

4. Prototyping

Interactive prototypes for user testing

5. Visual Design

High-fidelity mockups with branding

Key Features

Easy Booking

Streamlined ticket booking process with clear station selection, fare calculation, and multiple payment options.

Train Timing

Real-time schedule information with next train arrivals and platform details.

GPS Tracking

Live tracking of train locations with estimated arrival times at each station.

Route Planning

Smart route suggestions with transfer information and travel time estimates.

Digital Tickets

QR code-based digital tickets that can be scanned at entry gates.

Notifications

Alerts for delays, platform changes, and service disruptions.

Results & Impact

After implementing the redesigned interface in user testing:

Booking Time

Reduced from 2.5 minutes to 45 seconds

User Satisfaction

Increased from 3.2/5 to 4.7/5

Error Rate

Decreased by 78% during ticket purchase

The redesigned Chennai Metro application successfully addressed user pain points and created a more intuitive, efficient experience for metro travelers in Chennai.
