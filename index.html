About Me.

I'm Akilesh Hariharan, a UI/UX Designer and Developer based in Chennai, India, with over 2 years of experience in designing and building intuitive, user-centric digital products. My work bridges the gap between design and development, allowing me to create seamless, responsive experiences that are not only visually appealing but also technically sound and scalable.

As a designer, I specialize in creating wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity UI mockups using tools like Figma, Adobe XD, and Photoshop. I've led the UI/UX design of mobile apps and web platforms, focusing on user flow, interaction patterns, and accessibility. My strong visual design foundation, supported by over 3 years of part-time graphic design experience, enables me to craft engaging interfaces and brand-aligned visuals that resonate with users.

As a developer, I bring designs to life with clean, maintainable code, ensuring pixel-perfect implementation across devices. I'm proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript (ES6+), and React.js, and I frequently work with Tailwind CSS, Vite, and Reactbits to create dynamic, responsive interfaces. I also have basic backend knowledge in PHP and Node.js, which helps me collaborate efficiently in full-stack environments.

At SmartZEN Solutions Pvt Ltd, I’ve worked on projects that span cloud deployment, SEO enhancement, and digital marketing strategy—giving me a holistic view of how great design contributes to business impact. My strength lies in owning the entire UI/UX process—from research and design to front-end development and optimization.

Driven by curiosity and a continuous desire to improve, I aim to create digital experiences that are functional, elegant, and user-focused—whether it's a mobile app interface, a responsive web layout, or a complete product experience.
